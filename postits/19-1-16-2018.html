Security is a process of assessing and prioritising strategies for risk management. Consider questions like what are management likely forms of attack and what are common methods that have happened previously. We also learned about a common, classic buffer called Memory Safe. I liked her teaching style in that the professor went showed a basic function for hacking an airplane ticket program and had us keep raising the defence.
