So I dusted off my old site and started restructuring a bit.

During my last (and notably first) consistent blog, I wrote daily about evyerthing I was learning. In a Medium post, I said I wanted to write about how I'm growing, scaling and learning. But I think I'm actually going to dedicate this blog to a mixture of stuff that happens in my life: how I'm doing, what I'm learning, where I'm walking, when I'm thinking and who I'm becoming.
