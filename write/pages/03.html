My role as a Support Engineer goes far beyond product mastery. Products are updated regularly updated or at their environments or dependencies are at least. Either way, something is going to change at some point, so mastery is really an abritrary depth of knowledge. I'm not focused on the what nearly as much as I'm focused on the why and the how. I aim for understand a product throughout it's entire life cycle and, in turn, how that affects the customer experience.

I've had the pleasure to work with UX researchers, designers, engineers and customers to gain a comprehensive understanding of the product from ideation to creation to implementation. Having developed a full understanding of the customer’s workflow from product to contact, I’ve been able to provide thorough explanations, spot and accurately bugs faster and ultimately resolve issues faster for our customers.

As the customer experience is an evergreen task, I'm beginning to expand these symbiotic relationships by creating feedback systems with the customer's experience back to the engineering and product teams to create a more customer-centric product line.
